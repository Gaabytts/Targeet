SEO Search Engine Optimization

SEO - Search Engine Optimization

SEO (Search Engine Optimization), also known as website optimization, is quickly defined as a way to increase your site's hits through a set of techniques and strategies that allow a site to improve its positioning in the optimized results of the search engines such as Google and Bing.

What does SEO provide?

When you start an optimization campaign on your site, the improvement in the choice for your business keywords is directly proportional to the number of hits you will receive, as well as generating greater visibility for the brand and a return on site conversions.





GOOGLE ADWORDS

Google Adwords

AdWords is Google's advertising platform. Through this service, ads are displayed to users in the form of sponsored links when someone performs a search, whether through computers or smartphones. Ads appear primarily in search results, related to the keywords used in this search.
